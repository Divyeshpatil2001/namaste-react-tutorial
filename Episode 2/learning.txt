6 difference between caret and tide in package.json?
- "parcel": "^2.12.0" = (^)caret
- "parcel": "~2.12.0" = (~)tide
- caret means automatic updates the minor version. (2.5 - 2.6 or 2.7)
- tide means automatic updates the major version.  (2.5 - 3 or 3.5)
